Event Snapshot

A concise summary of the program

This international gathering is built for university leaders, faculty innovators and digital transformation teams who need both strategic direction and practical implementation ideas for AI-ready higher education.

Program window

29 June to 1 July 2026

Three structured days covering leadership, pedagogy, assessment, immersive learning and regional exchange.

Venue

Universiti Kebangsaan Malaysia

Hosted at Universiti Kebangsaan Malaysia with optional Kuala Lumpur immersion activities on the closing day.

Format

Keynotes, forum and workshops

Opening remarks, keynote sessions, a plenary forum, hands-on workshops and an optional excursion.

Regional mix

Malaysia and beyond

Speakers and cases connect Malaysia, the Philippines, India and Bangladesh through higher education transformation practice.

Co-Hosted By

Partner institutions supporting the program

AUAP-UKM Digital & AI Transformation Program

Hosted by Universiti Kebangsaan Malaysia, this program convenes higher education partners around sustainable, future-focused digital transformation.
